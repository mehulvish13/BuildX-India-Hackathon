# BuildX India Hackathon

BuildX India Hackathon 2026 is a national-level innovation challenge organized by UDTech India and powered by Unstop. This hackathon aims to bring together students, developers, innovators, and problem-solvers from across India to build impactful solutions using technology.

Participants will work on real-world challenges in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Sustainability, and Open Innovation.

This event provides an opportunity for participants to showcase their creativity, technical skills, and innovative thinking while competing with talented individuals from across the country.

Hackathon Tracks:
• Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
• Web & App Development
• Sustainability & Smart Solutions
• Open Innovation

Eligibility:
• Open for students, developers, and tech enthusiasts across India
• Participants can join individually or in teams
• Maximum team size: 4 members

Hackathon Format:

Round 1 – Idea Submission
Participants will submit their project idea including problem statement, solution approach, and expected impact.

Round 2 – Prototype Development
Shortlisted teams will build a working prototype or MVP of their solution.

Round 3 – Final Presentation
Finalists will present their solution to the judging panel.

Evaluation Criteria:
• Innovation & Creativity
• Technical Implementation
• Practical Impact
• Presentation & Clarity

Rewards & Benefits:
• Exciting prizes for top teams
• Participation certificates for all participants
• Networking with innovators and developers
• Opportunity to showcase your ideas at a national level
